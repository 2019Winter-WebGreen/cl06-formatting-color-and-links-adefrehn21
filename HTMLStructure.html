Padua Academy

All about Padua Academy and what makes it unique!

Padua Academy is located in Wilmington, Delaware on 905 North Broom Street. You can contact us at (302) 421-3739. Our Mission Statement is: Rooted in the Catholic faith, Padua Academy offers young women a transformational college preparatory education, challenging them to live Christ-centered lives of leadership and service.

We offer a variety of classes at three different levels including, CP (College Prep), Honors, and AP (Advance Placement). We also offer a variety of athletic programs including all types of sports: Cross Country, Basketball, Soccer, Volleyball, Softball, Lacrosse, and many more! We are are rooted in Sisterhood, Spirituality, Scholarship, and Service throughout our time at Padua Academy.
